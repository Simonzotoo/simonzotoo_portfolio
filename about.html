About Me

I’m Simon Zotoo a developer, data analyst, and student leader. I’m currently pursuing a BSc in Information Technology at Wisconsin International University College, Ghana, where I also serve as the SCOTSA President.

I enjoy building systems that make life easier; from banking systems in Java to management tools in PHP and data dashboards in Power BI. I’m not just learning to code, I’m learning to solve real-world problems.

I’ve worked with the SSNIT Head Office as a database intern, helped manage student performance systems, and built apps like a birthday reminder that keeps track of what you forget.

Outside of tech, I play piano at church, I lead when it counts, and I believe in always showing up with purpose.
